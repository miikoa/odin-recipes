Ingredients

  • 1 baguette tradition (mi-cuite)
  • 2 slices of medium thick ham
  • 2 tbls of salted butter

Preparation

  1. Open bread horizontally but not all of the way
  2. Spread butter generously on both sides of bread.
  3. Add ham

    4. Enjoy!!
