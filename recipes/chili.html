Chili

Everyone and his mother loves chili. Am I wrong? Well, as winter has just begun, chili is on our mind. To make a big pot of chili isn't rocket science. All it takes is basic, high quality ingredients and a little bit of love. There are as many chili recipes as there are people walking this earth but most will agree that all chili recipes have a core base of ingredients such as kidney beans, tomato sauce and spicy sauce. From there, the sky is the limit. If you'd like for me to hold your hand, check out the step by step information below.

This recipe will feed a family of 5.

List of Ingredients

  • 4 cloves of freshly minced garlic
  • 1 large grated white onion
  • 1 generous handful of freshly chopped herbs of choice
  • 2 large cans of stewed tomatoes
  • 2 large cans of kidney beans
  • 2 heaping tablespoons of smokey paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of celery salt
  • 1 fresh lime

How to Prepare

  1. add olive oil to the Dutch Oven
  2. add garlic and onions to the pot
  3. saute ingredients on a medium heat until they become translucent
  4. add fresh herbs and mixed well
  5. add all of the remaining ingredients, except the lime
  6. mix all of the ingredients well and after squeeze the entire lime over the pot
  7. mix and allow chili to bubble lightly for 40 minutes

Consider serving with homemade jalepeno cornbread.
