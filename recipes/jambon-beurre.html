Jambon Beurre

This recipe is one that is simple and a great crowd pleasure. Imagine that you have a whole gaggle of friends coming over for drinks or imagine waking-up in the middle of the night with hunger clawing at your belly? Well, this delicious sandwich is one that you add to your recipe rotation. The key to this recipe as with most is the quality of the ingdredients. Be sure to purchase items at a bakery, creamery and butcher shop. If that is not realistic, simply select quality ingredients from your local grocer.

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette tradition (mi-cuite)
  • 2 slices of medium thick ham
  • 2 tbls of salted butter

Preparation

  1. Open bread horizontally but not all of the way
  2. Spread butter generously on both sides of bread.
  3. Add ham

    4. Enjoy!!
